    So let's talk about

    My current project


    This project aims to generate fair and balanced teams from a list of players with different skills and characteristics. Using a user-friendly interface, users can enter the player information and the application uses this data to create balanced teams. The user interface allows for easy interaction where users can enter the players and their skills. Once entered, they are presented with a visual representation of the generated teams, allowing them to check the distribution of players and their allocation to teams.


    The program takes into account various factors such as the strengths and weaknesses of individual players, their positions and possible synergies between team members. It also offers the user the option to adjust or regenerate the teams if necessary to ensure that the teams remain fair and balanced.


    How did I come up with this idea? There is a discussion with my friends every week about who needs to be in a team with whom to keep it "roughly" balanced, I want to save myself the discussions and let the algorithm do the work :)
